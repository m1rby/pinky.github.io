Safety Tips

We care about your safety. Here are some important recommendations to ensure that your meetings and acquaintances always remain comfortable and protected.

  • 🔒 Never share personal information (such as address or financial information) with strangers.
  • 📍 Meet only in public places for the first meeting.
  • 📞 Tell friends or family where you are going and with whom.
  • ⚠️ Be wary of suspicious requests or behavior.
  • 💬 Use the platform to communicate before meeting in person.
  • 🚨 Report violations or suspicious profiles to the administrator.
